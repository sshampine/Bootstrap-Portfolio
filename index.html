About Me

My interest in computers started in the fourth grade. Our school had on Tandy TRS-80 that was shared between all classes. My fifth grade teacher purchased two TI-99/4a computers out of her own pocket just for her class. I guess she saw I was interested in them and made me their 'gatekeeper'. My first program was in BASIC and printed Hello! on the screen.

Now I'm a full-fledged system administrator who maintains corporate servers and storage. I'm ready for a change though. Typing in strange, colorful commands on a keyboard and making things appear on the screen is clever. Much more clever than turning a computer off then turning it back on again.
