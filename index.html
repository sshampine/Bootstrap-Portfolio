About Me

My interest in computers started in the fourth grade. The school had a TRS-80 that was shared between all classes. My fifth grade teacher purchased two TI-99/4a computers out of her own pocket for her class. I guess she saw I was interested in them and made me their 'gatekeeper'. Mrs. Cook saw the sys admin in me. My first program was in BASIC and printed Hello! on the screen.
